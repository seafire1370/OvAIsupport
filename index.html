About OvAI: Your On-Device AI Companion

Please note: OvAI is an independent application and is not affiliated with Google.

Experience the power of advanced AI right on your device with OvAI! This app brings the cutting-edge Gemma 3n E4B large language model, developed by Google, directly to your fingertips. Get instant responses, creative ideas, and intelligent assistance for a wide range of tasks, all powered by a lightweight yet sophisticated AI.

OvAI is designed to be user-friendly and efficient, providing a seamless and private conversational experience. Whether you need quick answers, assistance with writing, or just a conversational partner, OvAI is your go-to companion.

Key Features

  • Powered by Google's Gemma 3n E4B: Harness the capabilities of a state-of-the-art, open large language model.
  • On-Device Processing: Enjoy fast and private AI interactions. Your data stays on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and responsiveness.
  • Intelligent Assistance: Get help with writing, brainstorming, answering questions, and more!
  • Intuitive Interface: A clean and simple design built with SwiftUI for a seamless user experience.
  • Online Search Features: Using an online searching API, we are able to add online search data to our AI for up-to-date information.

About the Technology

OvAI is built upon the robust foundation of the open-source project 'gemma3n-ios'. We believe in transparency and the power of open-source development. You can explore the core framework and functionalities that drive this app on GitHub.

The AI capabilities within this application are powered by the Gemma 3n E4B model. Please note: OvAI is an independent application and is not affiliated with Google. This is a lightweight, high-performance open model from Google, designed for responsible and versatile use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does OvAI require an internet connection?

A: While OvAI's core LLM functionality operates on-device, it utilizes an online search API for up-to-date information. Therefore, an internet connection is recommended for full functionality, but the core AI interactions can still work offline.

Q: Is my data private with OvAI?

A: Yes, your direct conversations and data with the Gemma LLM run locally on your device and are not stored on external servers. For online search features, standard data handling practices for search queries apply to the respective search API.

Q: How do I update the Gemma LLM model?

A: Updates for the Gemma LLM model and the app will be provided through standard app updates. Please ensure your OvAI app is always updated to the latest version to get the best performance, features, and model improvements.

Q: I'm experiencing an issue or have feedback, what should I do?

A: Please refer to the "Contact Us" section below for our support details. We're here to help and appreciate your feedback!
